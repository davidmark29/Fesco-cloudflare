What is FESCO?

If you’re living in Faisalabad or one of the neighboring areas, your electricity provider is likely FESCO. This company is responsible for delivering electricity to more than 4 million customers. And with that many people depending on them for power, you'd better believe they’ve streamlined their services—including online billing.

Step-by-Step Guide to Check Your FESCO Bill Online

Check FESCO Bill Online is as easy as making a cup of chai (well, sort of). Let’s walk through the process.

Checking your FESCO bill online is a quick and easy process. Simply visit the our websitre at www.fescoonlinebil.pk and find the “Bill Inquiry” section. Once there, enter your 14-digit reference number, which can be found on any previous bills. After typing it in, hit “Submit” and your bill will be displayed on the screen. From here, you can view it, take a screenshot, download it, or print it for future reference. It’s as simple as that!

Bonus Tips for Checking Your Bill Online

  • Bookmark the Page: Save time by bookmarking the FESCO bill page.
  • Set Reminders: Set a reminder on your phone to check your bill a few days before the due date.
  • Check for Errors: If something doesn’t look right, call customer service for clarification.

How to Pay Your FESCO Bill Online

You can pay your bill online via:

  • Internet Banking: Log in to your bank’s internet banking service and select the option to pay your utility bill.
  • Mobile Banking: Apps like JazzCash or EasyPaisa allow for easy bill payment.
  • ATM: Use your bank’s ATM to pay your FESCO bill.
